This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how I-World Networks ("I-World," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal data when you use our internet services, website (www.iwn.ng), and related applications. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and securely.

verified_user By using our Services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect personal data to provide, improve, and secure our Services. The types of data we may collect include:

Account and Contact Information

When you sign up for or use our Services, we collect essential information like your name, physical address, email address, and phone number. This data is critical for establishing and managing your account, billing, and communication.

Service Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our Services (IP addresses, connection times, data consumption, and traffic patterns). We do not inspect the content of your communications beyond what is necessary for network management.

Device Information

We automatically collect technical information about the devices you use, including device type, operating system version, and unique identifiers, to ensure compatibility and provide accurate support.
