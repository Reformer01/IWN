Nigeria Google for Education Program Program

The Nigeria Google for Education Program (NGFEP) is powered by I-World Networks along with other technical partners. It is an initial $10 Million USD investment program dedicated to advancing digital learning in the Nigerian education sector, fully covering infrastructure and training at no cost to the institution.

Helping K-12 and Higher Institutions develop world-class digital learning infrastructure, change management processes, training, and capacity development. We launch schools into world-class Digital Learning Centers using the Google for Education learning platform.
