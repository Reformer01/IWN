Age Well Discount - Terms & Conditions

The Age Well Discount is subject to the following terms and conditions:

  1. Available to subscribers aged 60 years and above.
  2. Valid government-issued ID showing date of birth must be provided for verification.
  3. 15% discount applies to the monthly subscription fee only.
  4. Discount cannot be combined with other promotional offers unless explicitly stated.
  5. The discount will be applied automatically to qualifying accounts starting June 1, 2025.
  6. Existing customers who qualify will have the discount applied to their next billing cycle after June 1, 2025.
  7. I-World Networks reserves the right to modify or discontinue this program at any time with 30 days' notice.

Relationship Reward - Terms & Conditions

The Relationship Reward program is subject to the following terms and conditions:

  1. Loyalty is calculated based on continuous, uninterrupted service with I-World Networks.
  2. Customers with 3+ years of continuous service receive a 10% discount on their monthly subscription.
  3. Customers with 4+ years of continuous service receive a 15% discount on their monthly subscription.
  4. Any service interruption due to non-payment or voluntary suspension may reset the loyalty period.
  5. The discount applies to the base subscription fee only and does not include add-on services or equipment fees.
  6. I-World Networks reserves the right to modify or discontinue this program at any time with 30 days' notice.

Bundle and Save - Terms & Conditions

The Bundle and Save program is subject to the following terms and conditions:

  1. 10% discount applies when a customer maintains multiple active accounts under the same name or entity.
  2. All accounts must be in good standing to qualify for the discount.
  3. The discount applies to each qualifying account's monthly subscription fee.
  4. The discount will be applied automatically to qualifying accounts starting June 1, 2025.
  5. If one account is terminated or falls into arrears, the discount may be removed from all linked accounts.
  6. I-World Networks reserves the right to modify or discontinue this program at any time with 30 days' notice.

Rate Lock-down - Terms & Conditions

The Rate Lock-down offer is subject to the following terms and conditions:

  1. Customers must renew their subscription for a minimum of 6 months between December 1, 2024, and May 31, 2025, to qualify.
  2. The locked rate applies only to the subscription plan active at the time of renewal.
  3. Any plan changes or upgrades after renewal will be subject to the new pricing structure.
  4. The rate lock guarantee is valid for the duration of the renewed contract period only.
  5. This offer cannot be combined with other promotional discounts unless explicitly stated.
  6. Limited time offer that may be withdrawn at any time before May 31, 2025.
  7. I-World Networks reserves the right to modify the terms of this offer with appropriate notice.

Referral Bonus - Terms & Conditions

The Referral Bonus program is subject to the following terms and conditions:

  1. Existing customers in good standing are eligible to participate in the referral program.
  2. The referred customer must be new to I-World Networks and must mention the referrer at the time of sign-up.
  3. The free month credit equals one month of the referrer's current subscription plan value.
  4. There is no limit to the number of referrals a customer can make.
  5. Credits cannot be converted to cash and will be applied to future billing cycles.
  6. I-World Networks reserves the right to modify or discontinue this program at any time with 30 days' notice.

Prepaid Savings Discount - Terms & Conditions
