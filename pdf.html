Payment Guide

Paying your I-World invoices through our Portal

1
Visit portal.iwn.ng (Chrome browser is recommended)
2
Login using your registered email and password.
Support: support@iworldnetworks.net www.iwn.ng
3
From the dashboard, under the Account balance section, click on the Pay by Paystack button.
4
Click on "Pay by Paystack" on the redirected page.
Billing: accounts@iworldnetworks.net portal.iwn.ng
5
Click on "Pay Securely with paystack" on the redirected page.
6
Authorize payment with your preferred method and wait for confirmation.
Important Notice Your account will be credited once payment is successful. If unsure, check your dashboard balance or contact support for failed transactions.
