What is Google Workspace?

Google Workspace was rebranded from Google G Suite in 2020, rebranded from Google Apps for Work in 2016. Google Workspace is a platform trusted by more than 5 million businesses, enabling them to work faster, smarter, and more collaboratively every day.

This includes small businesses doing big things to enterprise businesses like Verizon, Colgate-Palmolive, Gant & Morrisons. In the past year alone, 1 million new customers have subscribed to Google Workspace.

The solution is a cloud-based business platform that enables businesses to have the flexibility to work from any location, at any time and from any device. Full details of the Google licenses and pricing plans are available from Google direct – Google licensing is straightforward.

Frequently Asked Questions about our Google Workspace Support Services

We often receive questions about our services or specifically Google Workspace for UK businesses so we thought we would clear them up with some helpful FAQs below. Of course, you can also pick the phone up and speak with us as we would be more than happy to help with.

Yes, Google WorkspaceWHAT IS GOOGLE WORKSPACE?

Google Workspace is a collection of powerful and secure cloud-based productivity and collaboration applications. Google Workspace acts as a centralised location for business tooling to combine disparate business solutions such as Google Workspace email, office suite, file storage etc. As a full collaborative suite, there are customisable tools integrated into the platform to effectively run and collaborate on IT operations from any location, at any time and on any device.

WHAT IS INCLUDED IN GOOGLE WORKSPACE?

Basic features include access to Google Suite, emails with domains, meetings and security. Depending on the package you choose, extra features include access to BigQuery, and data loss prevention applications.

HOW MUCH DOES GOOGLE WORKSPACE COST?

There are price plans to suit every budget, and our starter plan in the cheapest option available.

IS GOOGLE WORKSPACE FOR EDUCATION FREE?

Yes, Workspace for education is free, and it will remain that way.

WHAT’S THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN WORKSPACE AND GMAIL?

Gmail is a free and basic email application but Workspace offers a lot of extra features, including cloud storage, business emails, administrative controls and much more – depending on your chosen plan.

CAN I UPGRADE MY GMAIL TO GOOGLE WORKSPACE?

Yes, you can. CloudTech24 specialises in Google migration services, as the process can be a bit difficult. We guarantee maximum effectiveness and know you’ll be satisfied with the result.

HOW MUCH STORAGE WILL I GET?

Basic plans start at 30GB storage, but each package offers more storage. Our enterprise plan provides unlimited storage for all users.

WHY SHOULD I CHOOSE GOOGLE WORKSPACE OVER MICROSOFT 365?

Many people prefer using Workspace because it packs a host of features but has a simpler interface. If you’d like to migrate from Microsoft to Google, our team of specialists can create a seamless transition.

IS GOOGLE WORKSPACE FREE?

You can trial Google Workspace for free for 14 days (free trial period), however, there are costs associated with Google Workspace licensing. There are four subscription tiers: Google Workspace Basic, Google Workspace Standard, Workspace Business Plus and Google Workspace Enterprise. You pay per user, per month. CloudTech24 can assist you with choosing the best subscription and potential Google Workspace license discounts.

HOW ABOUT FOR CHARITIES?

Yes, Google Workspace is free for charities that need IT. It’s similar to basic but has features including shared drives available.

IS GOOGLE WORKSPACE SECURE?

Google Workspace is an incredibly secure cloud collaboration solution. Organisations can consolidate multiple platforms into a single business suite – thus not only simplifying data management and reducing the possibility of user error but securing the login authentication with built-in options for 2-factor authentication (2FA) or 2-Step-Verification (2SV). All data within Google Workspace is secured with “data at rest” and “data in motion” by default. Workspace also has great built in security protection including spam filtering to protect your organisation against advanced cyber security threats.

HOW DO I GET DIRECT GOOGLE TECHNICAL SUPPORT?

You can get in touch with Google’s admin support number at (650) 253-0000 or look for help on their Workspace portal.

WHAT IS A GOOGLE RESELLER?

A Google Reseller provides clients with Google Licensing and Management services – CloudTech24 can provide Clients with Google Workspace licensing, discounts and promotion codes in addition to ongoing technical support where our team can support your Workspace instance..

IS GOOGLE WORKSPACE EMAIL ENCRYPTED?

Google Workspace customers’ data is encrypted when it’s on a disk, stored on backup media, moving over the Internet, or travelling between data centres. Encryption is an important piece of the Google Workspace security strategy, helping to protect your emails, chats, Google Drive files, and other data.
