Nigeria Google For Education Program

The Nigeria Google For Education Program (NGFEP) is powered by I-World Networks along with other technical partners.
It is an initial 10 Million USD investment program in the Nigerian education sector to help K-12 and Higher Institutions develop world class digital learning infrastructure, change management process, training and capacity development. This will launch schools into world class Digital Learning Centers using the Google for Education learning platform.

Project Cost covered by Nigeria Google for Education Program on your campus.
