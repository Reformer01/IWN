I-WORLD NETWORKS PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: September 2025

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how I-World Networks ("I-World," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal data when you use our internet services, website (www.iwn.ng), and related applications. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and securely. By using our Services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect personal data to provide, improve, and secure our Services. The types of data we may collect include:

Account and Contact Information

When you sign up for or use our Services, we collect essential information like your name, physical address, email address, and phone number. This data is critical for establishing and managing your account, billing you for services, and communicating important information with you.

Service Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our Services to ensure our network operates efficiently and to troubleshoot issues. This includes data such as your IP addresses, connection times, the amount of data consumed (data usage), and traffic patterns. This helps us optimize network performance, manage congestion, and identify potential security threats. We do not inspect the content of your communications or the specific websites you visit beyond what is necessary for network management.

Device Information

We automatically collect technical information about the devices you use to access our Services, including the type of device (e.g., mobile phone, desktop computer), operating system version, and unique device identifiers. This information helps us ensure our Services are compatible with a wide range of devices, provide accurate technical support, and understand how our network is being accessed.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your personal data for the following legitimate business purposes:

To Provide Services

We use your data to deliver reliable internet services to your location, maintain your connection, and troubleshoot any technical issues that may arise. This includes using your account and device information to diagnose and resolve problems with your service.

Customer Support

When you contact us for assistance, we use your information to verify your identity and provide efficient, personalized support. This allows us to quickly access your account details and service history to resolve your queries or technical problems effectively.

Network Management and Security

We use Service Usage Data to monitor and manage our network to ensure fair and stable access for all users. This includes identifying and mitigating security threats, such as Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks, unauthorized access attempts, and the spread of malicious software, ensuring the integrity of our systems and the safety of our customers.

Service Improvement

We analyze aggregated and anonymized usage trends to better understand how our Services are used. This data-driven approach helps us identify areas for improvement, such as optimizing network infrastructure, developing new features, and enhancing the overall quality of our offerings.

Communication

We may use your contact information to send you essential service-related communications, such as notices about planned maintenance, changes to your account, updates to this Privacy Policy or our Terms and Conditions, and promotional offers that we believe may be of interest to you.

3. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf. This includes companies that handle payment processing, cloud hosting, and data analytics. These partners are legally and contractually obligated to protect your data and are only permitted to use it for the specific purposes we have authorized.

Legal and Regulatory Compliance

We may disclose your information if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or a valid legal process, such as a court order, search warrant, or government request.

Protection of I-World Networks

We may share data to protect the rights, property, or safety of I-World Networks, our customers, or the public. This includes sharing information to enforce our Terms and Conditions, investigate and prevent fraudulent activities, or respond to an emergency.

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement a comprehensive set of reasonable security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Our safeguards include technical measures such as encryption, access controls, and firewalls, as well as organizational measures like employee training and data handling policies. While we strive to protect your data with the highest standards, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we work diligently to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to:

Access

Request a clear and complete copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data we have on file for you, ensuring your information is always accurate.

Deletion

Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations that may require us to retain certain information.

Objection

Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes, such as direct marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to your request promptly and in accordance with all applicable laws.

6. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Policy on our Website and, where appropriate, through direct communication. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
