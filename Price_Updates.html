Important Service Update

Adjustments to I-World Networks Pricing

Dear Valued I-World Networks Customer,

Effective June 1st, 2025, our service pricing will be adjusted. This is necessary to maintain I-World Networks' reliable internet service in Nigeria.

This adjustment reflects the significant challenges facing the telecommunications sector, including:

  • The NCC's recently announced 50% tariff increase: This industry-wide change directly impacts our operational costs.
  • Rising fuel costs: Essential for our network maintenance and expansion.
  • Naira volatility: Increasing the cost of imported equipment and maintenance.
  • Continued infrastructure investments: To improve your internet experience amidst Nigeria's unique challenges.

We are committed to providing you with continued value. As part of this adjustment, we are offering up to 80% bandwidth increase at no additional cost! We understand that these changes may raise questions, and we are dedicated to providing you with the information you need. Please visit the following resources for more details:
Industry Changes: Learn more about the factors influencing our pricing.
FAQ Section: Find answers to frequently asked questions about the price adjustment.
Feedback Form: Share your thoughts and concerns with us.
Our customer support team is ready to assist you. Please do not hesitate to contact us at 07080217885, support@iworldnetworks.net, or via our online chat on our website.

We also offer flexible payment plans to assist with the price adjustment. Please see the FAQ section, or contact customer support for more information.
We appreciate your understanding and continued support as we work to provide you with the best possible internet experience in Nigeria.


Thank you for choosing I-World Networks,
The I-World Networks Team.
